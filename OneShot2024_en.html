Introduction

OneShot New Year's Eve Special Program is a large-scale program produced by OneShot fans and creators in China.
The program will be broadcast on Bilibili and YouTube on New Year's Eve every year.
We uphold the spirit of "Small circle, true love", and we are working hard to use our strength to illuminate this cold land.

Audit requirements

Contact us by the email address at the end of this post or send us a DM on our official Twitter account.

  1. Submission specifications are 1080p (1920×1080) at 60/30fps, without any black bars.
  2. If you want to apply for your own show, please read hereto the end, and contact us through the contact information at the end of the article, and attach your creative ideas and basis. After we have reviewed it, we will private message you if it is unanimously passed. If it is not passed, we will also explain the reason.
  3. Please note that we will not accept any works that have been released on any platform for more than one week (or have over 500 views). If the work has not yet reached the limit, you can delete the video. If we find any evidence of early uploading or reheating, the work will be removed from the program schedule.
  4. Based on the format of the program, we will not accept the following submissions for the time being:
    • Audio novels (novels are also not allowed, except for those that can be adapted into scripts or contain CG)
    • Submissions with excessive sarcasm, which would disrupt the overall structure and atmosphere of the program
    • Submissions with a large amount of text, such as exams, that are difficult to insert or even quote
    • Submissions that are collaborations with other games, but the content elements are not closely related to OneShot
    • Single paintings (except for those that include the painting process)
  5. Creators who have passed the review must guarantee that they can promptly (within one day) respond to messages from the administrators and provide as much advice as possible for the program. If we have any questions about your work in the later stages and need to make major changes, we will notify you. If you do not respond promptly after receiving the notification, or even delay the production process, you will be removed from the program schedule.
